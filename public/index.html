Who Are Our Donors?

Donors are individuals or organizations who selflessly contribute resources—whether money, food, or other essentials—to support those in need. Their generosity fuels positive change and fosters compassion.

Who Are Donee?

The donee refers to the recipient of a donation. In the context of a food donation website, the donee could be an individual, family, or organization in need that receives food contributions from donors.

Sponsorship

Sponsorship on a food donation website involves businesses or individuals providing support through funds or services, helping to sustain the site's mission of feeding the hungry.

About US

At Nourish Together, we believe in a world where no one goes hungry. Our mission is to connect surplus food with those in need, reducing waste and nourishing communities.
